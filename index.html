HOW TO PLAY?

KEY-IT: Get Ready to Test Your Skills!

Key-IT is a fast-paced game that challenges your
reflexes, typing skills, and coordination. Do you have what it takes?

Choose Your Difficulty

  • Mercy Mode: Get 3 lives to conquer increasingly challenging rounds.
    Make a mistake? No worries, you get two more chances!

  • Endless Mode: Push your limits! One wrong move and it's game over,
    but the faster you progress, the faster the game gets!

Challenge Categories

  • Click IT: Stars will appear on the screen. Test your reflexes
    by clicking on them before the timer runs out!

  • Type IT: Words will flash on the screen. Can you type them correctly
    before the timer expires?

  • Key IT: Look closely! An image of an arrow key pops up. Match the image
    by pressing the corresponding arrow key
    on your keyboard. Is it up, down, left, or right?

Stay Alert: A timer counts down for each category.
Running out of time costs a life, so be quick!
